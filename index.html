Understand what people value.

A domain name purchase is a high value user input that is succinct, carefully thought out and backed by a payment. Analysing the terms used in new domains is measuring a person's insight into topics that have value that they are willing to make a bet on.

Many domains are purchased with the intent to start something new and some domains are purchased with nefarious purposes, but in either case, they do so recognising a value.
